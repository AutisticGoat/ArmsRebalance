# ArmsRebalance: A mod for Cave Story+ (2026 update)
## ArmsRebalance is a mod for the upcoming Cave Story+ update for steam that does some changes to the arms in the game, mainly being an experiment

## Changes:

### Polar Star:
- Increased general shot duration
- Increased general damage:
  - Level 1: 1 > 2
  - Level 2: 2 > 4
  - Level 3: 4 > 6
- Maxed-out energy buffs:
  - Increased damage to 8
  - Removed 2 simultaneous shots limit
